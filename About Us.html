Vasuki Technocast: Your Partner for Superior Investment Casting.

Vasuki Technocast Private Limited

was established by a
team of skilled professionals.

with over two decades of experience in the field of investment casting.

As a leading manufacturer of steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, duplex steel, and nickel-based alloy castings, we utilize the lost wax process (investment casting) to produce high-quality components for various industries. Since starting production in April 2021, our facility has been equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and in- house testing facilities featuring the latest technology.

With a production capacity of 50MT per month and product weights ranging from a few grams to 70 kg, we meet global standards such as ASTM, DIN, EN, BS, JIS, and ASME. Our versatile product offerings cater to industries including oil & gas, marine, engineering, earthmoving, and automotive. At Vasuki Technocast, our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction drives us to deliver top-notch casting solutions that exceed the expectations of our diverse clientele.

At Vasuki Technocast, our vision is to become a leading exporter in the casting industry by consistently exceeding customer expectations while maintaining economic efficiency.

We are dedicated to investing in the latest technology and research to stay at the industry's forefront. By focusing on continuous improvement, we aim to deliver innovative solutions that keep our clients ahead of the competition.
