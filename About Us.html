Vasuki Technocast: Your Investment Casting Partner

Vasuki Technocast Private Limited

Is established by a
team of seasoned professionals

with over two decades of experience in the field of metal casting.

As a leading manufacturer of steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, duplex steel, and nickel-based alloy castings, we utilize the lost wax process (investment casting) to produce high-quality components for various industries. Since commencing production in April 2021, we have been equipped with state-of-the-art machinery in all process areas and in-house testing facilities, featuring the latest technology and testing instruments available in the market.

With a production capacity of 50MT per month and a single product weight range from a few grams up to 70 kg, we are fully equipped to meet global standards such as ASTM, DIN, EN, BS, JIS, and ASME. Our versatile product offerings cater to a wide array of industries, including oil & gas, marine, engineering, earthmoving, and automobile. At Vasuki Technocast, our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction drives us to deliver top-notch casting solutions that meet and exceed the expectations of our diverse clientele.

At vasuki technocast Our vision is to become a finest casting name in the industry by consistently exceeding our customer's expectations with maintaining economical efficiency.

We are committed to investing in the latest technology, research, and development to remain at the forefront of the industry. By focusing on continuous improvement, we strive to deliver cutting-edge solutions that keep our clients ahead of the competition.
